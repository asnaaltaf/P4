SIMPLE STRAWBERRY MOCKTAIL

"Oh, so sweet. Cheers! "

Course: Drinks    Cuisine: American
Prep Time: 5 minutes    Cook Time: 5 minutes    Total Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 2 people     Calories:97kcal


  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 lime or lemon
  • 2 cups soda water
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves


Directions

  1. Combine the strawberries, lime juice, basil, and sweetener to a bowl/jug and muddle well (or use a blender to puree).
  2. Optionally, strain the mixture through a fine-mesh strainer. Alternatively, simply add large ice cubes to the glass/es and top up with soda water. Stir Well.
  3. Optionally garnish with additional lemon/lime slices or even cucumber.
  4. Enjoy!
