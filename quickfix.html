BAKED PITA CHIPS

"A savory, crispy delight"

Course: Appetizer, Snack    Cuisine: American
Prep Time: 5 minutes    Cook Time: 10 minutes    Total Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 1 bowl    Calories:100kcal


  • 4 whole wheat pitas, cut into triangles
  • 2 ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dry basil
  • ¼ tsp salt


Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F degrees.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, mix olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, basil, pepper and salt.
  4. Place pita triangles in a bowl and pour seasoned olive oil over pita. Gently toss to coat each pita triangle, use your hands for this.
  5. Place pita triangles on prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until slightly browned.
  7. Cool and serve immediately!
