Chocolate Chip Pancakes Recipe

CHOCOLATE CHIP PANCAKES

"The sweetest, fluffiest comfort food"

Course: Appetizer, Breakfast    Cuisine: American
Prep Time: 5 minutes    Cook Time: 5 minutes    Total Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 8 pancakes    Calories:283kcal


  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 ¼ cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips


Directions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the milk, beaten egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix.
  4. Gently fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Heat a griddle over medium heat.
  6. Scoop ¼ cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges are set, flip!
  8. Serve with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
